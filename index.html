Welcome to Cheeky Spankos

We are a community dedicated to creating safe, fun, and consensual spanking parties for adults in New York City. Our events are a place for exploration, connection, and good-natured fun among like-minded people.

Whether you're new to the scene or a seasoned veteran, you'll find a welcoming atmosphere at our gatherings. We prioritize respect, consent, and the well-being of all our members above all else.

Explore our site to learn more about our upcoming events and our vetting process for new members. We look forward to meeting you!

Spanking Parties & General Extra-ness

We host spanking parties with a focus on school/related immersive events that allow adults the full headspace - a day of classes, homework assignments, tests, and of course - punishments if you misbehave. At night, we open back up to a broader party for anyone just looking for some spanking fun - but don’t be surprised if you peep someone expanding on the earlier shenanigans of the morning’s events. You know what they say: spanked at school, spanked at home. For more on the lore and structure of Cheeky Preporatory Academy, view our history page.
